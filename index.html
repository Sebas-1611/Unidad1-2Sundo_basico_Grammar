Part 5
Articles & Generalizations
13
Articles — Choose A, AN, THE, or Ø (no article)
📌 Rule A/AN = not specific, singular count noun  |  A before consonant sound · AN before vowel sound
THE = specific (we know which one, there's only one, or mentioned before)
Ø = no article with plural/non-count nouns for generalizations
Score: 0/6
14
Generalizations vs. Specific — Write G or S
📌 Rule Generalization: statement about all members of a group → "Bikes are fast." / "A bike is fast."
Specific: refers to a particular known noun → "The bike in the garage is fast."
